## Layout rules v1 (user-specified, empirically grounded)
Decoded from the 263-package corpus (1,221 iflws): activities 100×60,
gateways 40×40, events 32×32, participants 100×140; standard same-row
center-to-center pitch **150 px** (mode of 18,570 links).
- `_GW_STD = 150`; `_gw_layout` uses it for BOTH axes (rule 1+4: same step
  distance in X and Y, nodes centered in their slot).
- `_gw_route` + `_wp_xml`: orthogonal elbow edges. Fan-out (Router/Multicast)
  leaves the gateway vertically and turns onto the branch's own row; fan-in
  (Join/Gather) runs along the source row and enters the join vertically.
  Straight when shapes share a row/column. Applied to sequence flows (main +
  LIP), message flows, and `repair_flow_di`.
- Endpoint participants: center-aligned with their partner step, center
  exactly one pitch from the pool border on the nearest side (above/below for
  receivers, left for senders), 150 px stagger on collision; LIP endpoints
  hang one pitch below their LIP pool. Never touching, never negative coords.
